Website Goals-5,000 visitors in 6 months, 200+ wholesale inquiries (Year 1), 150+ monthly online sales by Month 8 AND 1,000+ newsletter subscribers
Key Features-Product showcase (Washing Powder, Car Wash Soap, Perfumes), Reseller & wholesale section, Car wash business partnerships,"Build Your Box” & subscription options AND Founder story and brand values
